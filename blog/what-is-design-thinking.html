What Is Design Thinking?

Written by Design Thinking, Explained

Published January 2026

Design thinking is a creative problem-solving process that focuses on understanding people first.

It is used in graphic design, product development, and digital design to create meaningful solutions.

The process often includes researching, brainstorming, prototyping, and testing ideas.
